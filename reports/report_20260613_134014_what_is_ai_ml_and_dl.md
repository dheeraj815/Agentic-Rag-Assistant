What are artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning!

## Answer
Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL) are distinct concepts in the field of computer science. 

1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**: AI is the broadest field, focused on creating systems that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence, such as reasoning, problem-solving, planning, and understanding natural language. It encompasses various approaches, including rule-based systems and data-driven approaches like ML and DL.

2. **Machine Learning (ML)**: ML is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed. It uses statistical models to analyze past data, detect patterns, and make predictions or decisions in new situations.

3. **Deep Learning (DL)**: DL is a subset of ML that uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers to automatically learn complex patterns from large datasets. It is particularly useful for handling unstructured data such as images, speech, and text, and can extract features on its own without needing human-defined rules.

In summary, AI is the umbrella term that covers all intelligent systems, ML is a technique that uses data to teach and enable systems to improve over time, and DL is a specialized form of ML that uses artificial neural networks to learn from large amounts of data.
